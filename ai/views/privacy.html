1. Introduction

Welcome to InfiNet AI ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI chat service at ai.infinet.services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Chat conversations and messages

2.2 Information from Google OAuth

If you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Google account email
  • Profile name
  • Profile picture

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Usage data and analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our AI chat service
  • Process your requests and transactions
  • Store and retrieve your chat conversations
  • Send you service-related communications
  • Improve our services
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your data securely using industry-standard encryption. Your chat conversations are stored in our database and are associated with your user account. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist in operating our service

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your chat conversations
  • Opt-out of certain data processing

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session and improve your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
