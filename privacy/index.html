InfiNet Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how InfiNet Services ("InfiNet", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you visit https://infinet.services, use the InfiNet Hub mobile application for iOS or Android, interact with our AI Studio tools, or otherwise engage with our services. It is designed to meet the disclosure standards required by Apple's App Store, Google Play, GDPR, and other global privacy frameworks.

Effective Date: 24 November 2025 Applies to: InfiNet website, InfiNet Hub mobile app, AI Studio Assistant & Creator suite

Quick Commitments

We built InfiNet Hub with privacy by design. These pillars guide every product decision.

Minimal data first

We collect only what we need to verify your identity, personalize workspaces, and provide AI-powered assistance.

No data sales ever

We never sell or lease personal data. Information is only shared with vetted processors that help us deliver the service.

Security obsessed

Passwords are hashed, data in transit is encrypted, and infrastructure is monitored 24/7 on Contabo-secured servers.

1. What Data We Collect

We collect information in three primary ways — directly from you, automatically through your devices, and from trusted partners. This section complies with App Store and Google Play Store disclosure requirements.

A. Information you provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name, phone number (optional), role/title, profile avatar image.
  • Authentication Data: Hashed passwords, email verification codes, password reset tokens, two-factor authentication codes.
  • User Content: AI chat messages and prompts, Creator form inputs (blog topics, social media posts, image prompts), uploaded images and files, voice recordings (for AI voice features), support tickets, feedback submissions.
  • Tools Usage Data: URLs processed for shortening, domains checked for availability, IP addresses looked up, files uploaded for conversion (images, documents), image files processed for resizing or favicon generation, QR code data, SEO preview inputs, business name keywords, color palette inputs, UTM parameters, speed test URLs.
  • Payment Information: Purchase confirmations, invoices, billing addresses (processed securely through third-party payment processors; we do not store full credit card numbers). Note: The app is free to use. Payment information relates only to manual invoice tracking and receipt uploads for Whish Money settlements.

B. Automatically collected (iOS & Android)

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version (iOS/Android), app version, unique device identifiers (IDFV on iOS, Android ID on Android), device language and region settings.
  • App Usage Data: Feature usage, navigation paths, timestamps, session duration, screen views, button taps, AI feature interactions.
  • Performance & Diagnostics: Crash logs, error reports, performance metrics, memory usage, network request logs.
  • Network Information: IP address, network type (WiFi/cellular), approximate geographic location (derived from IP), user agent, referrer URLs.
  • Mobile-Specific Data: Push notification tokens (for iOS and Android), Expo/React Native device identifiers, locally cached AI responses and generated content stored on your device.
  • Permissions Data:
    • Camera: Used only when you explicitly choose to take photos for image generation features (AI Creator tools). We do not continuously access your camera or record video. Photos are processed when you actively use the image generation feature and are not stored unless you choose to save them.
    • Microphone: Used for voice messages sent to the AI assistant. Audio is only recorded when you actively use the voice recording feature.
    • Photo Library: Used to save QR codes and generated images, and to select images for use in the app.
    • Storage: Used for file management, saving downloads, and caching app data.

C. From service partners

  • App Stores: Apple App Store and Google Play Store provide purchase status, subscription information, crash analytics, and app review data.
  • Email Services: Email delivery platforms (SendGrid, Twilio) provide delivery confirmations, bounce reports, and suppression lists.
  • Analytics Providers: App Store analytics (Apple App Store and Google Play Store) provide aggregated usage statistics and engagement metrics. We do not currently use third-party analytics services like Google Analytics.
  • AI Service Providers: Google Gemini API and OpenAI DALL·E receive prompt content and generation requests (see Section 5 for details).

2. How We Use Information

  • Account creation, sign-in, multi-factor email verification, and password recovery.
  • Operating the AI Studio Assistant, Creator (blog, social, prompt, and image requests), and automation blueprints.
  • Providing Web Tools Suite features (domain checking, QR code generation, file conversion, image processing, URL shortening, SEO preview, and other utility tools).
  • Routing prompts to Google Gemini models and OpenAI's DALL·E image endpoint, while limiting payloads to what is necessary for generation.
  • Processing files through our file converter service for format conversion (files are processed temporarily and deleted immediately after conversion).
  • Syncing activity history across devices, maintaining recent projects, and showing relevant recommendations.
  • Sending transactional emails, push notifications, and critical product announcements.
  • Monitoring uptime, detecting abuse, preventing fraud, and securing accounts.
  • Complying with legal obligations, enforcing policies, and responding to lawful requests.

3. Legal Bases for Processing

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or any jurisdiction that requires a legal basis, we rely on:

  • Contract necessity — to provide the services you request (projects, AI workflows, account management).
  • Legitimate interests — to protect users, improve products, and prevent abuse (balanced against your rights).
  • Consent — for optional marketing, analytics, and certain data sharing with AI partners.
  • Legal obligations — to maintain tax records, respond to government authorities, or comply with platform rules.

4. How We Share Information

We disclose personal data only when necessary to run InfiNet Hub or comply with the law. This disclosure meets App Store and Google Play Store requirements.

  • Service Providers & Infrastructure: Contabo (cloud hosting in EU), PM2 (process manager), Apache reverse proxy, database and storage vendors. These providers process data under strict contractual agreements.
  • File Converter Service: Files uploaded for conversion (images, documents) are processed by our file converter service hosted separately. Files are processed temporarily and deleted immediately after conversion. No files are stored permanently on the converter service.
  • AI & Creative Engines (Third-Party Data Sharing):
    • Google LLC (Gemini API): Receives chat messages, prompts, and conversation context to generate AI responses. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
    • OpenAI, LLC (DALL·E): Receives image generation prompts and parameters to create AI-generated images. OpenAI's privacy policy applies: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
    • We strip personally identifiable information from prompts when possible before sending to AI providers.
  • App Store Platforms: Apple App Store and Google Play Store receive app usage analytics, crash reports, and purchase data as part of their standard app distribution services.
  • Communications Services: Email and push notification platforms (Apple Push Notification Service, Firebase Cloud Messaging) deliver verification codes, alerts, and reports.
  • Third-Party Tool Services: Some Web Tools features use third-party APIs (e.g., IP lookup services). When you use these tools, only the necessary data (such as IP addresses for lookup) is sent to the third-party service. These services process data according to their own privacy policies.
  • Analytics & Diagnostics: Tools that help us measure feature adoption, crashes, and performance receive pseudonymized metrics (no personally identifiable information).
  • Compliance & Safety: We may share information when required by law, during security investigations, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, or financing.
Important: We never sell or rent personal data. We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers. All third-party sharing is limited to what is necessary to provide our services.

5. Web Tools Suite & File Processing

InfiNet Hub includes a comprehensive Web Tools Suite that processes various types of data. This section discloses how your data is handled when using these tools, as required by App Store and Google Play Store policies.

  • Tools Data Processing: When you use our Web Tools (domain checker, QR generator, file converter, image resizer, etc.), we process the data you provide (URLs, domains, IP addresses, files, images) to deliver the requested service. This data is processed temporarily and is not used for any other purpose.
  • File Converter Service: Files uploaded for conversion are sent to our file converter service for processing. Files are converted and immediately deleted from the converter service. We do not store your files permanently. The converted files are returned to you and cached locally on your device if you choose to save them.
  • Tools History: We maintain a history of your tool usage (which tools you used, when, and basic parameters) to provide you with a history feature in the app. This history is stored locally on your device and synced to our servers for cross-device access. You can clear this history at any time.
  • Third-Party Tool Services: Some tools (like IP lookup) may use third-party APIs. In such cases, only the necessary data (e.g., IP address for lookup) is sent to the third-party service, and their privacy policies apply.
  • Data Minimization: We only collect and process the minimum data necessary to provide each tool's functionality. We do not use tool usage data for advertising or marketing purposes.
Your Control: You can clear your tool history at any time from the app settings. Files processed through the converter service are automatically deleted after conversion.

6. AI Studio & Creative Tools (Third-Party AI Services)

To deliver AI-powered experiences, we use third-party AI services. This section discloses how your data is processed by these services, as required by App Store and Google Play Store policies.

  • Google Gemini API: When you use the AI Assistant chat feature or voice messages, your messages and conversation context are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate responses. Google processes this data according to their privacy policy. We do not share your account information with Google, only the conversation content.
  • OpenAI DALL·E: When you generate images using our Image Creator feature, your image prompts and parameters are sent to OpenAI's DALL·E service. OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy. Generated images are stored temporarily on our servers and cached in the app.
  • Data Minimization: We strip personally identifiable information (email, name, account details) from prompts whenever possible before forwarding them to AI providers.
  • Content Storage: Chat conversations and Creator prompts are temporarily logged to maintain conversation history, improve responses, and surface recent activity. You can delete this data at any time.
  • Image Caching: Image generation results are cached on our servers and within the mobile app until you save or clear them. You can clear cached content from the app settings.
  • Safety Measures: We enforce content filters and rate limits to prevent abusive or unsafe generations. We do not use your content to train AI models without your explicit consent.
Your Control: You can opt out of AI features at any time by not using the AI Assistant or Creator tools. You can also delete your conversation history and generated content from within the app.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data is stored for as long as your account remains active or as required by law.
  • Email verification codes, OTPs, and reset tokens expire within minutes and are purged within 30 days.
  • AI prompts, chat logs, and Creator outputs are retained until you delete them, clear cache, or request erasure.
  • Tools usage history (domain checks, QR codes, file conversions, etc.) is retained until you clear it from the app or request erasure.
  • Files processed through the file converter service are deleted immediately after conversion and are not stored.
  • Backups and server logs are retained for up to 12 months for disaster recovery and security auditing.

8. Security & Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, as required by App Store and Google Play Store security guidelines:

  • Encryption: All data in transit is encrypted using Transport Layer Security (HTTPS/TLS). Data at rest is encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods.
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and other industry-standard algorithms; we never store plain-text passwords.
  • Access Controls: Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel via SSH keys, multi-factor authentication, and role-based access controls.
  • Secure Infrastructure: Our servers are hosted on Contabo's secure EU data centers with regular security updates and monitoring.
  • Monitoring & Logging: Regular monitoring (PM2, Apache logs, custom alerts) detects suspicious behavior, security threats, and uptime issues.
  • App Security: The InfiNet Hub mobile app uses secure storage for sensitive data, certificate pinning for API communications, and follows iOS and Android security best practices.
  • Incident Response: We have procedures in place to respond to security incidents and will notify affected users and authorities as required by law.
Note: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your data to the best of our ability.

9.1. Account Deletion Request

InfiNet Services (InfiNet Hub) allows you to request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. This section explains how to request account deletion and what data will be deleted or retained.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your InfiNet Hub account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email request: Email us at admin@infinet.services with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your full name (as registered in your account)
    • A clear statement that you want to delete your account and all associated data
  2. Verification: We will verify your identity by sending a confirmation email to your registered email address. You must respond to confirm the deletion request.
  3. Processing: Once verified, we will process your account deletion request within 30 days (or sooner where required by law).

Data That Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your account profile information (name, email, phone number, avatar)
  • All projects and project assignments
  • AI chat conversations and prompts
  • AI-generated content (images, text, etc.)
  • Uploaded files and documents
  • Web Tools usage history
  • Support tickets and feedback submissions
  • Authentication tokens and session data

Data That May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for legal or operational reasons, as required by law or our legitimate business interests:

  • Financial records: Invoices, billing records, and payment information may be retained for up to 7 years as required by tax and accounting regulations.
  • Backup data: Data in backups may be retained for up to 12 months before permanent deletion.
  • Logs and analytics: Anonymized server logs and aggregated analytics data that cannot be linked to your identity may be retained for security and performance monitoring.
  • Legal obligations: Data required to comply with legal obligations, enforce our Terms of Service, or resolve disputes may be retained as necessary.

After Account Deletion

  • You will no longer be able to access your account or any data associated with it
  • All your projects, files, and AI-generated content will be permanently deleted
  • You can create a new account at any time, but your previous data cannot be restored
  • You will stop receiving push notifications and emails from InfiNet Hub
Contact Information:
To request account deletion, email us at admin@infinet.services with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
We will respond to your request within 30 days (or sooner where required by law).

9.2. Partial Data Deletion Request

InfiNet Services (InfiNet Hub) allows you to request deletion of specific data without deleting your entire account. This gives you more control over your personal information.

How to Request Partial Data Deletion

You can request deletion of specific data in the following ways:

  1. In-App Deletion: You can delete certain data directly from within the InfiNet Hub app:
    • AI Chat History: Open the AI Assistant, go to conversation history, and delete individual conversations or clear all history.
    • AI-Generated Content: Delete generated images, text, or other AI-created content from the Creator tools section.
    • Web Tools History: Clear your tool usage history from the app settings.
    • Uploaded Files: Delete files you've uploaded for projects from the file management section.
  2. Email Request: For data that cannot be deleted in-app, email us at admin@infinet.services with:
    • Your registered email address
    • Specify which data you want deleted (e.g., "Delete my project tracking history", "Remove my profile avatar", "Delete my support tickets")

Types of Data You Can Delete

The following data can be deleted without deleting your account:

  • AI Conversations: Chat history and prompts with the AI Assistant
  • AI-Generated Content: Images, text, and other content created using AI Creator tools
  • Web Tools History: History of domain checks, QR codes, file conversions, and other tool usage
  • Uploaded Files: Files and documents you've uploaded to projects
  • Profile Information: Avatar image, phone number (optional fields)
  • Support Tickets: Support tickets and feedback submissions (upon request)
  • Project Data: Individual projects and their associated data (upon request)

Data That Cannot Be Deleted Separately

Some data is required for account functionality and cannot be deleted individually:

  • Account Email: Required for account identification and login
  • Account Name: Required for account identification (though you can update it)
  • Authentication Data: Password hashes and authentication tokens (required for account security)
  • Account Creation Date: Required for account management

To delete these, you would need to delete your entire account (see Account Deletion Request above).

Processing Time

  • In-App Deletion: Immediate - data is deleted as soon as you confirm the action
  • Email Requests: Processed within 30 days (or sooner where required by law)
  • Backup Data: Data in backups may take up to 12 months to be fully purged
Contact Information:
To request partial data deletion, email us at admin@infinet.services specifying which data you want deleted, or use the in-app deletion features available in the app settings and respective sections.
We will respond to your request within 30 days (or sooner where required by law).

10. International Data Transfers

InfiNet operates from infrastructure located in the European Union (Contabo data centers in Germany). However, we use third-party services that may process data in other countries:

  • Google LLC (Gemini API): Processes data in the United States and other countries where Google operates. Google complies with GDPR and uses Standard Contractual Clauses for international transfers.
  • OpenAI, LLC (DALL·E): Processes data in the United States. OpenAI complies with GDPR and uses appropriate safeguards for international transfers.
  • Apple App Store & Google Play Store: Process app data according to their respective privacy policies and may transfer data internationally.

When personal data moves across borders, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), service-provider certifications, adequacy decisions, or comparable safeguards to ensure an adequate level of protection as required by GDPR and other data protection laws.
