Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, every website we build is fully responsive, meaning it automatically adjusts to look great on all screen sizes — including smartphones and tablets. We prioritize mobile-friendly design to ensure your visitors have a smooth and engaging experience, no matter what device they’re using.

How quickly can you deliver a website for our business?

Do you offer maintenance?

Yes! We offer 2 months of free maintenance after your website goes live. During this period, we’ll handle any minor updates, bug fixes, or support requests. If you need ongoing maintenance after that, we also offer affordable long-term support plans tailored to your needs.

What is a domain name and hosting?

A domain name is your website’s address (like yourwebsite.com) — it’s what people type into their browser to find you. Hosting is where your website’s files and data are stored so it can be accessed online. Think of it like your site’s home, while the domain is its street address.
